Who We Are

NULLGRIDS Labs is an education-focused cybersecurity initiative built to support schools in strengthening cyber safety awareness and preparedness.

We believe schools don't need fear-driven security solutions. They need calm, clear processes, age-appropriate education, and documentation that helps staff respond responsibly when digital issues arise.
